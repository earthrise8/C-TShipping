C+T Cargo Services

Our Services

Aboard the Rampion, we make biweekly trips to and from Luna, with a home port at New Beijing. We offer complete confidentiality and the fastest transport times between the Moon and Mars. No other shipping company has the same level of connections to both the Lunar Crown and the Eastern Commonwealth, through our connection with Emperor Kaito and Queen Selene.

The Rampion

The Rampion is a modified Rampion-class cargo ship and is the only privately owned ship of its class. It is a versatile and reliable vessel, capable of transporting a wide variety of cargo to fit your needs, from small, priceless family heirlooms to large, heavy equipment. It is equipped with military-grade, state-of-the-art navigation and communication systems, and is crewed by a team of experienced and dedicated professionals. The Rampion is the perfect choice for all your shipping needs.

Our History

C and T Cargo Transport was formed after Princess Selene reclaimed her throne from Lunar Dictator Queen Levana. For Captain Carswell Thorne and Crescent Darnell’s services to the Earthen Union and Luna, they were granted custody of the Rampion as a gift from the American Republic. Now, Carswell and Cress explore the system while transporting valuable cargo between the Earthen Union and Luna.

Our Team

Captain Carswell Thorne

Born and raised in Los Angeles, Carswell always dreamed of flying his own ship. A former pilot of the American Republic Military, he was essential in saving Princess Selene and restoring her to power. He serves as the pilot of the Rampion.

Crescent Darnell

Raised on a satellite in low Earth orbit, Cress is a Lunar shell and an expert in navigation and communications. Taken from her parents at birth, she adapted to long stints in space and became incredibly resourceful. She is the navigator of the Rampion.
