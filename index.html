The Rampion

The Rampion is a modified Rampion-class cargo ship, and is the only privately owned ship of its class. It is a versatile and reliable vessel, capable of transporting a wide variety of cargo, from small, delicate items to large, heavy equipment. It is equipped with a state-of-the-art navigation and communication system, and is crewed by a team of experienced and dedicated professionals. The Rampion is the perfect choice for all your shipping needs.

Our History

C and T Cargo Transport was formed after Princess Selene took her throne back from the Lunar Dictator Queen Levana. For Captain Carswell Thorne and Crecent Darnell's services to the Earthen Union and Luna, they were granted custody of the Rampion as a gift from the American Republic. Now, Carswell and Cress explore the world, while also transporting valuable cargo between the Earthen Union and Luna.
