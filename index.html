Our History

Formed after restoring Princess Selene to the Lunar Throne, Cress and Thorne Cargo was established to make shipping between the Earthen Union and Luna easier and accesable. Piloting the only civilian, Rampion class cargo ship, we specialize in transportation from the Eastern Commonwealth to the port of Artemesia on Luna.
