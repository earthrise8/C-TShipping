The Rampion

The Rampion is a modified Rampion-class cargo ship, and is the only privately owned ship of its class. It is a versatile and reliable vessel, capable of transporting a wide variety of cargo, from small, delicate items to large, heavy equipment. It is equipped with a state-of-the-art navigation and communication system, and is crewed by a team of experienced and dedicated professionals. The Rampion is the perfect choice for all your shipping needs.

Our History

Formed after restoring Princess Selene to the Lunar Throne, Cress and Thorne Cargo was established to make shipping between the Earthen Union and Luna easier and acessable. Piloting the only Rampion class cargo ship, we specialize in transportation from the Eastern Commonwealth to the port of Artemesia on Luna. Our strong connections with the governments of both nations enable us rapid and secure transportaion across the solar system.
